# PS5 Remote Play on iPhone and iPad with Pylux

Pylux is a free, open-source PS4 and PS5 Remote Play client for iPhone and iPad, available on the App Store.

## Download

[Download on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/pylux-remote-play/id6761292658){ .md-button .md-button--primary target="_blank" rel="noopener" }

## Setup

1. Install Pylux from the App Store
2. Follow the [Setup Overview](index.md) to register your PS4 or PS5 console
3. Configure [Remote Connection](remoteconnection.md) to connect over the internet (Internet Play)

## Features on iOS / iPadOS

- Touch controls optimized for iPhone and iPad
- Internet Play — stream your PlayStation from anywhere
- Automatic console discovery on your local network

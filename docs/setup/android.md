# PS5 Remote Play on Android with Pylux

Pylux is a free, open-source PS4 and PS5 Remote Play client for Android phones, tablets, and Android TV. It is available on the Google Play Store with automatic updates.

## Download

[Get it on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.pylux.stream){ .md-button .md-button--primary target="_blank" rel="noopener" }

Supports Android phones, tablets, and **Android TV** devices.

## Setup

1. Install Pylux from the Google Play Store
2. Follow the [Setup Overview](index.md) to register your PS4 or PS5 console
3. Configure [Remote Connection](remoteconnection.md) to connect over the internet (Internet Play)

## Features on Android

- Touch-friendly on-screen controls for phones and tablets
- Android TV support — use with a controller on your TV
- Internet Play — connect to your console from anywhere without port forwarding
- Automatic console discovery on your local network
